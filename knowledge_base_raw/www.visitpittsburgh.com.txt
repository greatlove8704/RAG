Pittsburgh Will Surprise You
Enjoy the sunshine and the start of spring in our city of multitudes Summer and sunshine are starting to make their way into the region, which means plenty of activities are starting to get outside. You can too with baseball season and different festivals and concerts throughout the city, or you can wait out the last bits of rain and cold with tons of indoor adventures still to be had.
Pittsburgh is on the clock! The 2026 NFL Draft is just around the corner, and we're more than ready to welcome thousands of football fans to the celebration. We're counting down the days until the NFL Draft officially hits our shores with a massive countdown clock! Its sleek yet gritty design perfectly captures what it means to be a Pittsburgher, and it knows it. On one side, it honors each of our 90 neighborhoods while on the other, it highlights the high school football teams from the WPIAL and Pittsburgh City Leagues. There's no better way to get hyped than seeing the number of days winding down, but don't just take our word for it. See the clock for yourself on the Great Lawn outside of Acrisure Stadium, and be sure to show up your photo ops!
